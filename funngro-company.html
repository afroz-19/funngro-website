FAQs

Everything Companies Ask

Are there legal considerations for working with teens? ▾
Yes. Funngro only works with teens aged 14 and above. We collect age verification and parental approval before any teen can apply to your projects. All payments are compliant with Indian labour and minor employment guidelines.
How is talent curated for my project? ▾
From a pool of thousands of teens, Funngro curates candidates based on experience, past project ratings, skill assessments, and commitment level. You receive a shortlist of the best-fit candidates.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work? ▾
Funngro has a quality assurance process. If the work doesn't meet standards, we facilitate revision requests. Payment is released only after you approve the final submission.
What categories of work can teens do? ▾
Currently 12 categories including content writing, social media, graphic design, market research, product testing, app/web development, video creation, data entry, and more. If your need isn't listed, reach out — we'll find a match.
How is payment handled? ▾
All payments flow through Funngro's secure platform. You deposit project funds upfront (held in escrow) and release payment only on work approval. Teens receive payment directly into their Funngro Wallet, withdrawable to UPI or bank.
